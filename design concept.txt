Building Connections BrainStorm
====================================
Cyst game
controls:
 - Player can WASD to move the camera around the map
 - Player can hit B to toggle building mode (Think starcraft for UI/UX)
 - Player can then hit a variety of hotkeys for other buildings
 
loop:
 - Place cysts to spread infestation (like creep in starcraft)
 - Resources are required to place down cysts or other buildings
 - Player gets attacked by random waves of mobs trying to purge the infestation, 
   progressively getting stronger (New enemies get introduced at certain time intervals)
 - Resources are gathered by corpses on the ground that are covered by infestation
 - Game ends when all buildings are destroyed, time survived is score
